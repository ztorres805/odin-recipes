Sourdough Bread

Description

This is a home recipe, tried and tested! Sourdough bread is not just delicious it is good for your gut.
With this recipe you will be able to create a fluffly delicate yet crunchy loaf of sourdough.

Ingredients

  • Sourdough Starter
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add Water to Starter and mix
  2. Add Flour
  3. Let Rise
  4. Strech & Fold
  5. Let Proof
  6. Shape dough into ball
  7. Cold Proof in Fridge
  8. Score
  9. Bake with lid (Dutch Oven)
  10. Remove lid and bake to color
  11. Let Rest & Enjoy!
